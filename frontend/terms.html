2. Description of Service

ResumeAI is an AI-powered resume optimization tool that helps job seekers tailor their resumes to specific job descriptions. The Service provides:

  • ATS compatibility scoring — a match score indicating how well your resume fits a job description
  • Keyword gap analysis — identification of missing keywords from the job posting
  • AI resume rewriting — suggested rewrites of your bullet points and summary to better match the role
  • Resume export — download your optimized resume as a PDF or DOCX file
  • Resume history — saved versions of your optimizations (registered users only)

This Service is currently a portfolio project and is provided for demonstration and personal use. Features and availability may change at any time.
