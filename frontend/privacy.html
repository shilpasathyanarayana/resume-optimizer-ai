1. Introduction

Welcome to ResumeAI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website. By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this document.

This website is currently a portfolio project. As we grow, features may be added — including advertising — and this policy will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review it periodically.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our resume optimization tools, we may collect:

  • Resume or CV files you upload for analysis or optimization
  • Your name and email address if you contact us or create an account
  • Any feedback, messages, or content you submit through the site

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address and general geographic location
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on the site, and navigation patterns
  • Referring URLs (the page you came from before visiting ours)
  • Usage analytics to help us understand how the site is used

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, operate, and improve our resume optimization services, to analyze usage patterns, and to respond to any enquiries you send us. We do not currently use your personal information for advertising purposes.

When we introduce advertising features in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated and you will be notified of any material changes.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather analytics data. Types we use include:

  • Essential Cookies — Necessary for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand how visitors interact with the site.
  • Preference Cookies — Remember your settings and preferences for future visits.

When advertising is introduced, we or our advertising partners may use additional cookies to serve relevant ads. You will be given the opportunity to manage your preferences at that time. You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Sharing Your Information

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers — Trusted third parties (hosting, analytics, email) who help operate the website. They are contractually required to keep your data secure.
  • Legal Requirements — Where required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers — If the website or its assets are acquired, your information may be transferred. You will be notified in advance.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party advertisers at this time. If this changes, we will update this policy and seek your consent where required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Resume files uploaded for optimization are processed and not stored permanently unless you have explicitly saved them to an account.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction, including secure server infrastructure, encrypted connections (HTTPS), and restricted access to user data.

No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access to your data, correction, deletion, objection to processing, data portability, and the ability to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you by email or a notice on the website.
